# Small Rituals

Grand transformations are overrated. Most of my life changed through **boring repetition**:

- Making tea before opening the laptop
- A walk without headphones on Sundays
- Closing the day by writing one true sentence

## Why rituals work

They reduce decision fatigue. They signal to the body: *we are in this mode now*. Engineers understand state machines. Humans are state machines with feelings.

## A ritual is not a cage

When a ritual becomes guilt, it has expired. The form can change. The intention remains — to mark time as something passing through you, not something you are drowning in.

I keep this chapter short on purpose. Stillness does not need many words.
