# Writing at Night

Daylight is for building systems. Night is for **admitting things**.

I notice a pattern: the commits I am proud of ship at 2pm; the paragraphs I am afraid of write themselves at 2am. Not because darkness makes me poetic — because the inbox is asleep and the inner critic is tired.

## What I write after dark

Sometimes it is code — a refactor that finally makes sense. More often it is a message I almost delete. A memory I do not want to lose. A question I am not ready to ask out loud.

## Permission

If you are reading this in the Letters part of my book, you are in the room where I allow softness. That is intentional. Not everything belongs in Engineering. Not everything should be optimized.

The heart has its own deployment schedule.
