# Listening to Users Without Losing the Plot

Users will tell you what hurts. They will rarely tell you what to build.

## How I take feedback

| What they say | What I hear |
| --- | --- |
| "Add feature X" | "I am anxious about outcome Y" |
| "This is broken" | "My trust wobbled here" |
| "Competitor has Z" | "I need reassurance you are serious" |

## The line I protect

Vision is not stubbornness. But if you chase every loud request, you will ship a **Frankenstein** with excellent Jira hygiene.

Listen deeply. Commit selectively.
