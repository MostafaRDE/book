# When to Say No

Every yes is a no to something else — usually sleep, coherence, or the one feature that would actually matter.

## Good reasons to refuse

- The request serves a single loud user, not a pattern
- It forks the mental model of the product
- You cannot explain the value in one sentence

## How to say it

With respect, with clarity, and with an alternative when possible. "Not now" is often more honest than "yes" followed by silence.
