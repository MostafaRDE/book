# Launch Day Is Not the Finish Line

Launch day feels like a cliff. In reality it is a **door** — and on the other side is maintenance, support, and the slow work of being believed.

## The three phases nobody posts about

1. **The hour after** — errors you could not simulate, congratulations you cannot fully feel
2. **The first week** — support tickets as product research
3. **The first month** — deciding what "success" means without Twitter's opinion

Ship anyway. Just do not confuse adrenaline with sustainability.
